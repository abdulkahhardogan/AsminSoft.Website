ContractorPass — Company Portal

Do You Face These Issues?

  • Collecting, submitting and manually tracking 10–14 documents per contractor employee
  • Different teams reviewing different documents under data-privacy regulations — no centralized coordination
  • Incomplete or incorrect documents requiring repeated submission and re-review, extending timelines
  • Tracking document expiry dates and keeping records current throughout employment
  • Difficulty meeting legal obligations (data privacy, HSE) without a centralized system

What It Does for Your Company

No More Late Starts

Contractor employees start on time — document delays no longer hold up work or cause financial losses.

Time Savings for Review Teams

Document reviewers work faster with a centralized digital workflow — less back-and-forth, more efficiency.

Data Privacy & Legal Compliance

Each team sees only the documents they are authorized to view. Data-privacy and HSE obligations are met systematically.

Instant Audit Readiness

All documents are instantly accessible when audits occur — no more scrambling through scattered records.

Corporate Reporting

Real-time reports on contractor headcount, document status and card validity — ready for management.

Full Digital Transformation

Replace paper and email with an online platform. Satisfaction increases on both the company and contractor side.

More Control with Fewer Staff

By automating repetitive document tracking, the system lets fewer people manage more contractor employees — delivering direct financial gains.

Who Is It For?

Construction sites, power plants, refineries, ports, industrial facilities, organized industrial zones, and any corporation with significant contractor workforce presence.
